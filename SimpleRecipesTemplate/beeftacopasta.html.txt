            <!--Recipe Name-->
            <h2>Beef Taco Pasta</h2>

            <!--Description-->
            <p>This is a simple but tasty recipe I love for taco seasoned beef pasta with salsa instead of tomato 
                sauce. It usuals gives me about 4-5 servings.</p>

            <!--Prep and Cook Times-->
            <h3>Prep and Cook Times</h3>
            <!-- <p>Prep Time: </p> -->
            <p>Cook Time: 20 minutes</p>

            <!--Ingredients-->
            <h3>Ingredients</h3>
                <ul>
                    <li>1lb Ground Beef</li>
                    <li>~2 Cups of Elbow Pasta or Pasta of Choice</li>
                    <li>1 Packet of Taco Seasoning</li>
                    <li>2/3 Cup of Water</li>
                    <li>1 16oz Jar of Salsa</li>
                    <li>Any shredded Cheese of Your Choice</li>
                </ul>

            <!--Instructions-->
            <h3>Instructions</h3>
            <p>1. Cook your pasta and beef.</p>
            <p>2. Follow the instructions of the taco seasoning, if given. If not, drain
                the beef and return to pan. Add 2/3 cup of water to the beef and add the seasoning.</p>
            <p>3. Bring the beef and water to a simmer until all the water is gone.</p>
            <p>4. Combine the pasta, beef and salsa, mixing thoroughly.</p>
            <p>5. Serve and top with cheese and garnish as desired.</p>
